Why Reading Builds Imagination

Reading allows children to explore worlds beyond their experience, imagine characters, and develop creativity. Encourage kids to read daily!

5 Books Every 10-Year-Old Should Read

Books help develop vocabulary and empathy. Some great picks are:
- Charlotte's Web
- The Trumpet of the Swan
- Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
- Matilda
- The Chronicles of Narnia

Fun Story Prompts for Young Writers

- Imagine a talking dog who solves mysteries.
- Write about a secret library hidden under your school.
- What if animals could send emails? Write a story!
