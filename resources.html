Books for Kids

Some recommended books for children aged 8–12:

  • Charlotte's Web – E. B. White
  • The Trumpet of the Swan – E. B. White
  • Matilda – Roald Dahl
  • Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone – J.K. Rowling
  • The Chronicles of Narnia – C.S. Lewis

Story Writing Prompts

Encourage kids to write their own stories using these prompts:

  • A secret library is discovered under your school.
  • Write about a talking dog who solves mysteries.
  • Imagine a day when animals can send emails.
  • Create a new adventure for Luna the girl detective and her dog Bruno.
